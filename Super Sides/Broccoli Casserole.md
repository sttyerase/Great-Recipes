
## Broccoli Casserole

##### Ingredients:

| Quantity          | Ingredient             |
|-------------------|------------------------|
| 1 stick           | butter                 |
| 1 pkg.            | Ritz crackers, crushed |
| 2 10 oz. packages | chopped broccoli       |
| 8 oz.             | Velveeta cheese, cubed |

##### Preparation:
Cook broccoli as directed on package.  Drain and place in casserole.  Pour half of the
butter over the broccoli.  Add cheese.  Mix remaining butter with the cracker crumbs and pour the
mixture over the broccoli.  Bake at 325&deg; for 20 - 30 minutes.
