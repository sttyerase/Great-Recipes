

## Corn Casserole

##### Ingredients:
| Quantity       |    Ingredient|
|----------------| -------------------------------------|
 | 1 can          | creamed corn|
 | 1 pkg.         | frozen corn|
 | 1 cup          | sour cream|
 | 2              | eggs, beaten|
 | 3 tsp.         | sugar|
 | 1 box          | Jiffy corn meal muffin mix dry|
 | 1 stick        | butter|

##### Preparation:
Preheat oven to 350&deg;.

Mix ingredients together.  Pour into greased casserole.  Bake 40-45 min.
