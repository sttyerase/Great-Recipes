
## SPINACH LASAGNA ROLL-UPS*

##### Ingredients:

| Quantity      | Ingredient                 |
|---------------|----------------------------|
| 8             | lasagna noodles            |
| 1 tbsp.       | olive oil                  |
| 2 large clove | garlic, minced             |
| 1-10 oz. pkg. | frozen spinach, chopped    |
| 1 cup         | ricotta cheese             |
| 1 cup         | shredded mozzarella cheese |
| 1 tsp.        | salt                       |
| 1-14 oz. jar  | spaghetti sauce            |
| 1/4 cup       | grated Parmesan cheese     |

##### Preparation:
Preheat oven to 350&deg; 

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.  Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water,about 8 minutes, 
stirring occasionally.  Noodles should be tender yet firm to the bite.  Drain.  Lay noodles flat
onto a flat surface.

Heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat.  Add garlic.  Cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
Transfer garlic to a large bowl.  Add spinach, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese , and salt to the bowl.
Stir gently until evenly combined.

Spread a heaping 1/4 cup of spinach mixture into the middle of each lasagna noodle.  Roll noodles firmly
around filling. Arrange in a baking dish.  Pour spaghetti sauce over the rolls. Cover dish with aluminum foil.

Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.  Remove foil and continue baking for 5 more minutes.  Sprinkle
Parmesan cheese over roll-ups to serve.
