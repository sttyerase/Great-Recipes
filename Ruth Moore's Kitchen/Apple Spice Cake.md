
## Apple Spice Cake

##### Ingredients:

| Quantity | Ingredient      |
|----------|-----------------|
| 2 cups   | sugar           |
| 1/2 cup  | shortening      |
| 2        | eggs            |
| 4 cups   | chopped apples  |
| 2 cups   | flour           |
| 1 tsp.   | baking soda     |
| 1/2 tsp. | salt            |
| 1 tsp.   | cinnamon        |
| 1/4 tsp. | allspice        |
| 1/4 tsp. | nutmeg          |
| 1/2 cup  | chopped walnuts |

##### Preparation:
Mix shortening, sugar & eggs.  Add chopped apples, then dry ingredients.  Add nuts last.  Bake at 350&deg;
for 45 min.
